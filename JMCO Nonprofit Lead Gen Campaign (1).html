Advertising Campaign Strategy

Search-Intent Campaigns

Include ad extensions highlighting 94% Net Promoter Score and expertise in single audits and UBIT compliance.

Educational Content Campaigns

LinkedIn and Facebook campaigns to custom audiences of nonprofit leaders, promoting valuable resources rather than direct sales.

Feature snippets from lead magnets while positioning James Moore as the go-to expert.

Retargeting & Lookalike Campaigns

Retarget website visitors with specific resources based on pages viewed. Create lookalike audiences from current nonprofit clients.
